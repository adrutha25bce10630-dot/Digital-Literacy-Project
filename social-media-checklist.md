5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student

|Do's|Don'ts|
|-|-|
|Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. |Don’t share sensitive personal details like address, phone number, or passwords.|
|Maintain privacy settings to protect your personal information.|Don’t engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.|
|Use social media for learning, networking, and sharing useful content.|Don’t post inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content.|
|Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.|Don’t waste excessive time scrolling—balance academics and social media use.|
|Be respectful and professional in comments, messages, and posts.|Don’t plagiarize or use others’ content without giving proper credit.|
